Take Action

Now what? Here are some options:


Join a Club

There are a bunch of student organizations that work to solve issues related to the climate crisis. Check out a some of the options available at UCSD here.

Donate

By donating, you can help fund research or grow movements. Two great options are the Clean Air Task Force, which strives for realistic climate change solutions through technological innovation and policy advocacy, or the Sunrise Movement, which takes a politically driven approach.

    


Do Your Part

Use what you've learned here. Vote for politicians who will take aggressive action to combat the climate crisis. Conserve energy, electricity, and gas whenever possible. Eat less meat. Support environmentally friendly businesses. Most importantly, have conversations and spread awareness.
